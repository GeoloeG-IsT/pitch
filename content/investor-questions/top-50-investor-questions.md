# Top 50 Investor & VC Questions on a Pitch Deck

## The Business
1. What problem are you solving, and why does it matter now?
2. How big is the market opportunity (TAM, SAM, SOM)?
3. Who is your target customer, and how well do you know them?
4. What is your unique value proposition?
5. Why is your solution 10x better than the status quo?

## Product & Technology
6. Can you give us a live demo?
7. What is your core technology, and is any of it proprietary?
8. How defensible is your technology (patents, trade secrets, network effects)?
9. What is your product roadmap for the next 12–18 months?
10. How do you handle data privacy and security?

## Business Model & Revenue
11. How do you make money?
12. What is your pricing model, and how did you arrive at it?
13. What are your unit economics (CAC, LTV, LTV/CAC ratio)?
14. What is your gross margin, and how does it evolve at scale?
15. What does your revenue look like today, and what is the growth trajectory?

## Traction & Metrics
16. What traction do you have so far (users, revenue, LOIs, pilots)?
17. What is your month-over-month or year-over-year growth rate?
18. What are your key KPIs, and how do you track them?
19. What is your churn rate, and what drives it?
20. Can you share cohort retention data?

## Market & Competition
21. Who are your direct and indirect competitors?
22. What is your competitive moat?
23. How do you win against incumbents with more resources?
24. What happens if a large player (Google, Amazon, etc.) enters your space?
25. What market trends or tailwinds support your thesis?

## Go-to-Market
26. What is your go-to-market strategy?
27. What are your primary customer acquisition channels?
28. What does your sales cycle look like?
29. How do you plan to scale distribution?
30. Do you have any strategic partnerships or key accounts?

## Team
31. Why is this the right team to build this company?
32. What relevant domain expertise does the founding team have?
33. How did the co-founders meet, and how do you resolve disagreements?
34. What key hires do you need to make in the next 6–12 months?
35. What is your equity split, and is it fully vested?

## Financials & Fundraising
36. How much are you raising, and what are the terms?
37. How will you allocate the funds from this round?
38. What milestones will this funding help you achieve?
39. What is your current burn rate and runway?
40. When do you expect to reach profitability (or next fundraise)?

## Risk & Challenges
41. What is the biggest risk to this business?
42. What has been your biggest failure or setback so far, and what did you learn?
43. What regulatory or legal risks do you face?
44. What assumptions in your model are you least confident about?
45. What keeps you up at night?

## Vision & Exit
46. Where do you see the company in 5 years?
47. What does the exit landscape look like (acquirers, IPO potential)?
48. Are there comparable exits in your space, and what were the multiples?
49. How do you think about building long-term value vs. short-term growth?
50. Why should we invest in you and not the next company pitching us today?
